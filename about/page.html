About me

About me

My design journey kicked off in 2018 when I boldly signed up for a graphic design bootcamp — mostly out of curiosity and mild confusion about what designers actually do all day. At first, I wasn’t sure if design and I were meant to be, but the moment I saw how complex ideas could be turned into simple, meaningful visuals, I was hooked faster than you can say “color palette.”

Fast forward to 2020, I stumbled upon UX/UI design — the magical land where creativity meets problem-solving. Suddenly, it wasn’t just about making things look pretty; it was about making them actually work. That’s when I knew I’d found my sweet spot, and honestly, I haven’t looked back (mostly because I’ve been too busy prototyping).

Beyond Design

Beyond Design

When I’m not designing, you’ll probably find me passionately yelling at my TV during an English Premier League match — purely for tactical reasons, of course. I’m also a big fan of spy series (because who doesn’t love a good plot twist?) and the occasional video game marathon.

These days, though, my favorite role is being a new dad. Nothing beats winding down after work with my family — diaper duty included. Those little, chaotic, joy-filled moments make the long design hours totally worth it and remind me why I love what I do.
